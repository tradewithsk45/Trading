Our Story

MAASAATHUVAAN was born from a simple belief: trading education should build skills, not sell dreams. Founded by Karthik Murugesan after years in corporate finance and active trading.

The name means "your dedicated companion"—we walk alongside you, ensuring each concept builds toward practical market competence.

Our focus: developing disciplined, resilient traders who understand that risk management is the foundation of lasting success.
